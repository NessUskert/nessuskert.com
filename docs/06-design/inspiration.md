- [ ] Decide on the overall visual style and mood.

I want my website to be very interactive. I want there to be the professional part on top, with links to all the separate pages that showcase my work. And then I would like underneath that on the index page there to be interactive stuff just to get to know me. Like, a favorite song, favorite movie, what I am watching now, etc. And I want a contact me space on the bottom. I also want a unique sign in section where people can register themselves on the website. It needs a nav bar on the top, and when you scroll down, on the bottom. I want them to see the nav bar on top the whole time. I want it to be a simplistic design with creamy white pastels and then light, desaturated colors for each page. The index will be kind of rainbow as it displays all of them.
