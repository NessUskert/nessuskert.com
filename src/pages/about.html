About the Project

You may be wondering why I decided to make a very elaborate personal website. Well, I have been reflecting on my past achievements and future goals, because of college, and realized that I want a place to store everything I did and will do in high school. I also want to teach myself how to use artificial intelligence to code, and hopefully leave the experience able to make a website of this caliber without any outside help.

This website is a collection of the projects, stories, and experiences that have shaped who I am. I hope it gives you a glimpse into my interests, my goals, the things I'm building, and the person I am becoming.

Also, if you like any of the art, stories, or projects I have done (at a professional level), feel free to reach out and ask for my help in anything you want to do. I can help you with selling a product or writing a book, just ask!

The Beauty Of A Teardrop

They quench the thirsting drought of your mind,
Prismatically holding all sight in an oval body,
Leaking out your eye like from a cemetery, spirit.

You fear the desire you have for their presence,
Scorning God in a faint prayer when your eyes remain arid, asking,
“Why won’t they just come?”
